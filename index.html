The Realza Story

Royalty in
Every Detail

Realza — from the Spanish word for "royalty" — represents the evolution of Chetak Grani Marmo from trusted stone suppliers to creators of extraordinary marble artistry.

Where Chetak stands for volume and reliability, Realza embodies emotion, exclusivity, and elevation. We craft pieces that don't just fill spaces — they define them.
